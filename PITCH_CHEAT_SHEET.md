# Intelligent Trading Assistant - Pitch Cheat Sheet

## 1. The "Hook" (The Problem)
"Investing in the Tunis Stock Exchange (BVMT) is currently a manual, disconnected experience.
- **Novice Investors** like 'Ahmed' are intimidated by complex charts and don't know what to buy.
- **Expert Traders** are overwhelmed by noise and miss critical signals in the mountains of data.
- **Regulators** struggle to detect market manipulation in real-time.
Existing tools are just data viewers—they don't offer *intelligence*."

## 2. The Solution
"Our application is not just a dashboard; it's an **Intelligent Decision Support System**.
It bridges the gap between raw data and actionable advice by using AI to analyze market behavior, sentiment, and individual risk profiles."

## 3. Functionalities (The "Demo" Highlights)
- **Smart Recommendations**: "Instead of just showing a price, we say 'BUY' or 'HOLD' based on your specific profile."
- **Market Sentiment**: "We read the news so you don't have to." (Simulated for MVP).
- **Anomaly Watch**: "We act as a watchdog, spotting unusual volume spikes instantly."
- **Portfolio Learning**: "A zero-risk paper trading environment to test strategies."

## 4. The AI & Tech Stack (The "Secret Sauce")
This is where you show technical depth:

| Component | Technology | How it Works |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Price Prediction** | **Machine Learning (Regression)** | Analyzes 10 years of history to forecast 7-day trends. |
| **Sentiment Analysis** | **NLP (Rule-Based)** | Scans news headlines for positive/negative keywords to score market mood. |
| **Decision Agent** | **Expert System** | A multi-factor engine that combines Trend, RSI, Volume, and Sentiment into a single score. |
| **Anomaly Detection** | **Statistical AI** | Uses Z-Scores (Standard Deviation) to flag events that are statistically improbable (outliers). |
